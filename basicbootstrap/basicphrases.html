Basic Phrases

Basic Japanese Phrases

Kon'nichiwa (こんにちは) - Hello

Nē (ねえ) - Hey

Sayōnara (さようなら) - Goodbye

Onegaishimasu (お願いします) - Please

Arigatōgozaimashita - (ありがとうございました) Thank you

Dōitashimashite - (どういたしまして) You are welcome

Hai (はい) - Yes

Iie (いいえ) - No

Sumimasen (すみません) - Excuse me

Gomen'nasai (ごめんなさい) - I am sorry

Tasukete! (助けて!) - Help!

Ohayō gozaimasu (おはようございます) - Good morning

Konbanwa (こんばんは) - Good evening

O-yasumi nasai (おやすみなさい) - Good night

Jā mata ne (じゃあまたね) - See you later

Kiotsukete (気をつけて) - Take care

Daijōbudesuka (大丈夫ですか) - Are you okay

Watashi wa ikimasu (私は行きます) - I’m leaving

Itte rashai (イテラシェイ) - Have a nice day

Dōzo haitte kudasai (どうぞ入ってください) - Please come in

Itadakimasu (いただきます) - I humbly receive/Thanks for the food

Phrases for Meeting and Greeting

Eigo o hanasemasu ka. (えいごをはなせますか.) - Do you speak English?

Koko ni eigo o hanaseru hito wa imasu ka. (ここに　えいごおはなせるひとはいますか.) - Does anyone here speak English?

Watashi wa nihongo ga sukoshi shika hanasemasen. (わたしは　にほんごがすこししか　はなせません.) - I only speak a little Japanese.

O-namae wa nan desu ka. (おなまえはなんですか.) - What is your name?

O-genki desu ka. (おげんきですか.) - How are you?

Genki desu. (げんきです.) - I'm fine. Thank you.

Oaidekite ureshī desu. (おあいできて　うれしいです.) - I am very glad to meet you.

Wakarimasen. (わかりません.) - I don't understand.

Nante iimashita ka. (なんていいましたか.) - What did you say?

Motto yukkuri hanashite kudasai. (もっと　ゆっくりはなしてください.) - Can you speak more slowly?

Yoku wakarimasu. (よくわかります.) - I understand you perfectly.
